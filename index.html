About

Visual Time Travel is a browser extension that captures and organizes a visual timeline of your browsing history. As you navigate the web, the extension automatically takes screenshots of the pages you visit, creating a visual record that makes it easy to find and revisit important information.

Unlike traditional text-based history, Visual Time Travel gives you a visual snapshot of each page, making it easier to recognize and recall the content you've seen before. It's like having a photographic memory for your web browsing!

Features

Automatic Screenshot Capture

Automatically captures screenshots when you navigate to new tabs, creating a visual record of your browsing activity.

Visual Timeline

Browse your history with an intuitive visual timeline that makes it easy to find and revisit important pages.

Search & Filter

Quickly find what you're looking for with powerful search and filtering options based on date, domain, or keywords.

Secure Storage

All your data is stored securely in a MongoDB database with proper authentication and encryption.

Customizable Settings

Control when screenshots are captured with customizable settings to suit your preferences.

Cross-Browser Support

Works seamlessly across Chrome, Firefox, and Edge browsers for a consistent experience.

Installation

1

Download the Extension

Clone or download the repository from GitHub.

2

Open Extensions Page

Navigate to your browser's extensions page:

  • Chrome: Go to chrome://extensions/
  • Edge: Go to edge://extensions/
  • Firefox: Go to about:debugging#/runtime/this-firefox
3

Enable Developer Mode

Enable Developer Mode by toggling the switch in the top-right corner (Chrome/Edge) or clicking "This Firefox" in the sidebar (Firefox).

4

Load the Extension

Click "Load unpacked" (Chrome/Edge) or "Load Temporary Add-on" (Firefox) and select the extension directory from the downloaded repository.

5

Set Up Backend (Optional)

For full functionality, set up the backend server by following the instructions in the repository's README.

How to Use

1

Sign Up or Log In

Click on the extension icon in your browser toolbar and create an account or log in to your existing account.

2

Browse Normally

The extension will automatically capture screenshots as you browse the web. No additional action is required.

3

View Your Timeline

Click on the extension icon and select "View Timeline" to see your visual browsing history.

4

Search and Filter

Use the search bar and filters to find specific pages based on date, domain, or keywords.

5

Revisit Pages

Click on any screenshot in your timeline to revisit that page in a new tab.

6

Adjust Settings (Optional)

Click on the extension icon and toggle screenshot capturing on/off as needed.
