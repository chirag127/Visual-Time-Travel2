Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 15, 2023

Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for Visual Time Travel ("we", "our", or "the extension"). This policy explains what information our browser extension collects, how it is used, and the measures we take to protect your privacy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your browsing experience remains secure. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

By installing and using the Visual Time Travel extension, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Required Permissions

Visual Time Travel requires certain browser permissions to function properly. Here's why each permission is necessary:

tabs

This permission allows the extension to detect when you switch between tabs or navigate to new pages. It's essential for capturing screenshots at the right moment and associating them with the correct URL and page title.

activeTab

This permission gives the extension temporary access to the currently active tab, allowing it to capture a screenshot of the visible content. Without this permission, the extension would not be able to create the visual history.

storage

This permission allows the extension to store your preferences (such as whether screenshot capturing is enabled) and authentication information locally in your browser. This ensures your settings persist between browser sessions.

host permissions (http://*/*, https://*/*)

These permissions allow the extension to capture screenshots on any website you visit. Without these permissions, the extension would not be able to function on most websites.

Information We Collect

Visual Time Travel collects the following information:

  • Screenshots: The extension captures visual snapshots of the webpages you visit.
  • URLs: The web addresses of the pages you visit are recorded to associate them with the corresponding screenshots.
  • Page Titles: The titles of webpages are stored to help you identify pages in your visual history.
  • Timestamps: The date and time when you visited each page are recorded to organize your browsing history chronologically.
  • Favicons: The small icons associated with websites are stored to help you identify the source of each page.
  • User Preferences: Your settings for the extension, such as whether screenshot capturing is enabled.

What we DO NOT collect:

  • We do not collect your personal information beyond what is necessary for authentication (email).
  • We do not track your browsing behavior for advertising purposes.
  • We do not access or store your passwords, form data, or other sensitive information that may appear on webpages.
  • We do not analyze the content of the pages you visit beyond capturing the visual representation.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected by Visual Time Travel is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To create and display your visual browsing history timeline.
  • To enable you to search and filter your browsing history.
  • To allow you to revisit previously viewed pages.
  • To improve the functionality and user experience of the extension.

We do not:

  • Sell or rent your data to third parties.
  • Use your data for advertising purposes.
  • Share your data with any third parties except as described in this Privacy Policy.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in the following ways:

  • Local Storage: Your preferences and authentication information are stored locally in your browser using the browser's secure storage API.
  • Remote Storage: Screenshots and associated metadata (URLs, titles, timestamps) are stored in a MongoDB database. This allows you to access your visual history across different devices and ensures your data is not lost if you uninstall the extension.

We take the security of your data seriously and implement the following measures:

  • All communication with our servers is encrypted using HTTPS.
  • Authentication is handled securely using JSON Web Tokens (JWT).
  • Access to the database is restricted and protected by strong authentication mechanisms.
  • Regular security audits and updates are performed to ensure the continued protection of your data.

Third-Party Services

Visual Time Travel uses the following third-party services:

  • FreeImage.host: We use this service to store the screenshots you capture. When a screenshot is taken, it is uploaded to FreeImage.host, and only the resulting URL is stored in our database. Please refer to FreeImage.host's Terms of Service for more information about their data handling practices.
  • MongoDB Atlas: We use MongoDB Atlas as our database service to store metadata about your browsing history. MongoDB Atlas implements industry-standard security measures to protect your data. You can learn more about their security practices in their Privacy Policy.

User Rights

As a user of Visual Time Travel, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view all your captured screenshots and associated data through the extension's timeline interface.
  • Control: You can toggle screenshot capturing on or off at any time through the extension's settings.
  • Account Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us through GitHub issues.

Please note that Visual Time Travel is designed to maintain a permanent record of your browsing history. There is no built-in functionality to delete individual history items, as this would defeat the purpose of the extension. If you need to clear your history, you will need to request account deletion.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
  • Displaying a notification within the extension when you open it after a policy update.

We recommend that you review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through:

  • GitHub Issues - Create a new issue in our repository with your question or concern.

We will make every effort to address your concerns promptly and thoroughly.
